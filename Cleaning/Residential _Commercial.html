Residential & Commercial Cleaning

✔ Residential Cleaning: Residential cleaning focuses on homes, apartments, and private properties. Services typically include dusting, vacuuming, mopping, kitchen and bathroom sanitation, window cleaning, and organizing spaces. The cleaning frequency can vary from daily, weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly, depending on the homeowner's preferences. Residential cleaning often requires attention to detail, especially in areas like kitchens and bathrooms where germs can accumulate quickly. Eco-friendly and non-toxic products are preferred for the safety of families and pets.

✔ Commercial Cleaning: Commercmtial cleaning caters to businesses, offices, schools, healthcare facilities, and other public spaces. It involves larger-scale cleaning tasks, including floor maintenance, carpet cleaning, restroom sanitation, trash removal, and high-touch surface disinfection. Commercial cleaners often work during non-business hours to avoid disrupting operations. The use of industrial-grade equipment and cleaning agents is common to handle high-traffic areas efficiently. Regular deep cleaning is crucial for maintaining professional appearances and ensuring a healthy work environment for employees and clients.
