Full House Cleaning

✔ House cleaning involves a series of tasks aimed at making your home clean, fresh, and organized. Start by decluttering: clear any unnecessary items from countertops, tables, and floors. Then, dust surfaces like furniture, shelves, and baseboards, using a microfiber cloth or duster to capture dust.

✔ Next, clean surfaces: wipe down countertops, sinks, and appliances in the kitchen and bathroom. Pay attention to mirrors, faucets, and toilets in bathrooms. Vacuum carpets and rugs throughout the home, making sure to get under furniture. For hard floors, sweep and mop to remove dirt and grime.

✔ Clean windows with glass cleaner, and wipe down window sills to remove dust. Empty trash bins in all rooms, replacing liners with fresh bags.

✔ In the kitchen, focus on cleaning appliances like the stove, microwave, and refrigerator (inside and out). Replace towels in bathrooms and bedrooms, and fluff pillows to keep things neat.
For a deeper clean, consider tackling areas like baseboards, ceiling fans, and air vents. Finally, ensure everything is organized and tidy, leaving your home fresh and welcoming.
